# FresherFlow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-22

FresherFlow is a job and walk-in opportunity discovery platform for freshers and students in India. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

---

## Data we collect

### Account and profile
- Email address and display name (via Firebase Authentication — Google Sign-In or email/password)
- Profile details you provide: name, graduation year, degree, branch, skills, preferred job roles, and location preferences
- Username chosen during onboarding

### Job interactions
- Opportunities you save, apply to, or mark as not interested
- Job alerts and notification preferences you configure
- Companies you follow

### Usage data
- Device push notification token (for sending job alerts — stored server-side, deleted when you disable notifications or delete your account)
- Referral code if you joined via a referral link

### Feed and scoring
- Job match scoring (match score between your profile and opportunities) is calculated entirely on-device after the feed is downloaded. Match scores are not transmitted to our servers.
- The bootstrap feed is fetched from our CDN and cached locally on your device.

---

## How we use your data

- To show you relevant job and walk-in opportunities based on your profile and preferences
- To send job alert notifications for keywords and roles you subscribe to
- To power the referral system if you invite others
- To improve feed quality and opportunity matching (aggregated, not individual)

---

## What we do not do

- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not serve ads
- We do not share your profile with recruiters without your explicit action (e.g. applying)
- We do not track your device beyond what is needed for push notifications

---

## Data retention

- Your profile and interaction data is retained while your account is active.
- Deleting your account removes all personal data from our database within 30 days.
- Push notification tokens are deleted immediately on account deletion.

---

## Your rights

- You can view and edit your profile at any time in the app.
- You can delete your account from the app settings — this removes all your data.
- You can disable push notifications at any time from your device settings or the app settings.
